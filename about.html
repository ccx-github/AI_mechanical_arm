Our Story

The four of us all enjoy playing sports, and interact with those who share our interests. When we were chatting, we discovered by chanve that many people suffer from a long recovery time after hand injuries. During that time, they would not be able to play sports anymore and the recovering equipment is very annoying. Since we all know something about robotics and computer science, we decided to work together to develop this product.
