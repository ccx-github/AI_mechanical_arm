Key Statistics Supporting Our Innovation

1. Stroke Impact: Stroke affects over 15 million people annually, with 5 million left with permanent disabilities. Hand dysfunction is one of the most common impairments in stroke patients.

2. Home Rehabilitation Growth: There is a growing demand for home-based rehabilitation solutions, particularly in underserved regions and among aging populations.

3. AI's Role in Healthcare: AI-driven rehabilitation devices have been shown to improve recovery outcomes by providing real-time personalized therapy, reducing therapy time, and improving patient engagement.

4. Cost of Stroke Rehabilitation: The cost of stroke rehabilitation is a significant burden on healthcare systems worldwide. AI-powered, home-based solutions like RevoHands can help reduce costs by lowering the frequency of in-clinic visits and providing an effective alternative for rehabilitation.
