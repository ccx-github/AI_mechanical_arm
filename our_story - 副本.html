Our story

At RevoHands, our journey began from a shared passion for technology, innovation, and sports. During casual conversations, we realized that many people struggle with long recovery times after hand injuries, often preventing them from engaging in activities they love, such as sports. Traditional rehabilitation devices are often inconvenient, difficult to use, and lack the personalization needed for effective recovery. This insight sparked our determination to create a better solution.

With our combined expertise in robotics, computer science, and business, we decided to collaborate and bring our vision to life. Jayden, with experience in business planning and management, guided the company’s strategic direction, ensuring our vision aligned with real-world needs. Owen, with a background in economics and finance, managed the financial strategy, keeping the company sustainable as we grew. Alex, with a passion for design and programming, took the lead in technological development, applying his skills to create an innovative product. Meanwhile, Jiamu, with strong ties to local healthcare networks and a keen interest in research, focused on marketing and customer engagement, ensuring we remained close to the needs of our patients.

Together, we envisioned a device that could revolutionize rehabilitation, offering a solution that is not only effective but also accessible and personalized. Our mission became clear: to make rehabilitation easier, more efficient, and truly tailored to each individual, improving both the physical and emotional well-being of patients worldwide. Through this collaboration, RevoHands was born—a solution that we believe will empower people to reclaim their independence and enhance their quality of life.
